About The Artist

The Sound of Darkness

Scared The Living Icon is a musical project that emerged from the shadows, blending haunting melodies with deep, introspective lyrics. The artist's identity remains mysterious, allowing the music to speak for itself.

With influences ranging from darkwave to post-punk, the sound creates an atmospheric experience that transports listeners to another realm. Each track is carefully crafted to evoke emotion and tell a story.

The project began in 2018 and has since gained a cult following for its unique sound and aesthetic. Scared The Living Icon has released three full-length albums and numerous singles, each exploring different facets of the human experience through a dark, poetic lens.
